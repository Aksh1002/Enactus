ANKURAN

Launched as a measure to counter the perpetually rising levels of air pollution, Project Ankuran aims to bring down noxious pollutants in the immediate environment.
Vertical Gardens, made from completely recycled materials, and an easy to maintain panel ensures low maintenance and enhances the aesthetics. With a diverse community of carpenters, gardeners and artisans , Ankuran, is as vibrant towards the community as it is in it's benefits!
